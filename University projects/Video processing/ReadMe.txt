
This course was offered at Yazd University during the Spring 2024 semester, under the instruction of Dr. Ghani. The primary textbook for the course is Video Processing and Communication, published by Prentice Hall (link provided below).

Course exercises are implemented using both Python and MATLAB. The assignments are available in both Persian and English formats.
